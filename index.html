Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with the app?
After installation, simply open the app and follow the on-screen instructions. The setup process will take just a few minutes.
The app doesn't work on my device
Make sure you have the latest version of iOS installed. The app supports iOS 12.0 and later. Try restarting your device and reinstalling the app.
How do I restore my purchases?
Go to the app settings and tap "Restore Purchases". Use the same Apple ID that was used to make the original purchase.
Is my data safe?
We take data protection seriously. All data is encrypted and stored following security best practices. We never share your personal information with third parties.
How do I cancel my subscription?
You can cancel your subscription through your iPhone Settings > Your Name > Subscriptions > [App Name] > Cancel Subscription. The cancellation will take effect at the end of your current billing period.
